ASTER

What does ASTER measure?

ASTER captures information in visible (VNIR), shortwave infrared (SWIR), and thermal infrared (TIR) bands. This enables material discrimination, moisture detection, and thermal property analysis.

With stereo pairs, it generates digital elevation models, estimates land surface temperature, and tracks land-use/land-cover change.

    Observation capabilities

  • Topography (VNIR stereo pair)
  • Land surface temperature (TIR)
  • Land cover and land-use change
  • Minerals and moisture (VNIR/SWIR)

Covers visible, near/short-wave infrared and thermal infrared.
