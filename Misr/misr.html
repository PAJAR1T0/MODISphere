Multi-angle Viewer

Technical Sheet

9 Cameras
Angles: ±70°
Resolution: ~275 m
Orbit: Terra (705 km)
Coverage: Global

Why Multiple Angles?

Multi-angle observation enables calculation of cloud height using stereo triangulation and reveals the 3D structure of the atmosphere.

It also improves the characterization of aerosols and the BRDF (Bidirectional Reflectance Distribution Function), describing how surfaces reflect light depending on angle.
