What does MODIS measure?

MODIS measures radiance across 36 bands, from visible to thermal infrared. It enables global monitoring of vegetation, ocean color, surface temperature, clouds, aerosols and more.

Near-IR bands track vegetation water content, while thermal bands retrieve sea-surface temperature and detect fires.
